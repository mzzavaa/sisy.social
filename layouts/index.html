Why SISY Exists

Women are underrepresented on stages, in media, and in leadership - not because of ability, but because of broken discovery and visibility infrastructure.

For Women

  • Less visible than male peers despite equal or greater expertise
  • Underrepresented on panels and at conferences
  • Lack structured guidance for building visibility

For Organizations

  • Want diverse speakers but struggle to find them quickly
  • Repeatedly invite the same known profiles
  • Cannot easily filter experts by industry, language, or expertise
