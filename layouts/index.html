Our Story

Sisy was born from a simple observation: so many talented, intelligent women hold back from speaking up — not because they have nothing to say, but because they've never had a safe space to practice. We created Sisy to change that.

The Background

Research shows that women are interrupted more often, spoken over in meetings, and less likely to raise their hand — not because of ability, but because of conditioning. We wanted to build a place where that pattern breaks.

The Idea

What if speaking wasn't something you had to be "naturally good at" — but a skill you could practice in a community that actually wants you to succeed? That's the core idea behind Sisy: speaking is a muscle, and we train it together.

Our Values

Courage over Perfection

This space is for practice, not performance. No interruptions, no shaming.

Respect Always

Support over Competition
