About SISY

Relentless,
purposeful progress.

The name SISY references the myth of Sisyphus - never-ending work - reframed as relentless, purposeful progress toward equal visibility for women experts.

Vision & Mission

SISY increases the visibility of women as experts, speakers, and leaders across all industries. It enables women to showcase their knowledge, build public profiles, and access opportunities such as conferences, panels, media contributions, advisory roles, and collaborations.

By structuring expertise into discoverable profiles and supporting professional positioning, SISY helps women develop their voice and authority in their respective fields - and helps organizations discover diverse voices beyond existing networks.

Long-Term Vision

SISY envisions a world where:

Equal Representation

Women are equally represented on stages, in media, and in leadership.

Discoverable Expertise

Highly qualified female voices are easy to discover and invite.

Balanced Panels

Organizations have the tools to build genuinely balanced panels.

Public by Default

Expertise is structured, searchable, and public by default.

Our Story

The Background

Women are interrupted more often, spoken over in meetings, and invited less frequently to speak - not because of ability, but because of systemic visibility gaps. Highly qualified female voices exist in every industry, but the infrastructure to discover and surface them is broken. SISY was built to fix that.

The Idea

What if expertise was structured, searchable, and public by default? What if organizations had real tools to build genuinely balanced panels? What if the path from "qualified expert" to "recognized voice" was a supported journey, not an accident? That's what SISY builds.

Two Complementary Pillars

SISY is two complementary things sharing the same name and vision.

SISY Platform

A product/SaaS platform that helps women become visible as experts, speakers, and leaders across all industries. Expert profiles, opportunity matching, visibility support, community, and organizer tools.

Learn more

Sisy Framework

An open-source automation framework that packages repeatable patterns from the freelance automation ecosystem into a reusable setup. SisyCore, SisyCLI, SisyAPI, SisyBench, and SisyBot.
