SISY Platform

Female Speaker &
Expert Visibility

A product and SaaS platform that helps women become visible as experts, speakers, and leaders across all industries.

The Problem SISY Solves

For Women

  • Less visible than male peers despite equal or greater expertise
  • Invited less frequently to speak publicly
  • Underrepresented on panels and at conferences
  • Not recognized as experts despite strong experience
  • Hesitate to present themselves publicly
  • Lack structured guidance for building visibility
  • Rely heavily on personal networks for opportunities

For Organizations

  • Want more diverse speakers but struggle to find them quickly
  • Repeatedly invite the same known profiles
  • Lack structured ways to discover new voices
  • Need support building balanced panels
  • Cannot easily filter experts by industry, language, or expertise

Platform Components

Everything women need to go from hidden expert to recognized voice - and everything organizers need to find them.

Expert Profiles

Structured, public profiles that highlight each woman's full scope of expertise and professional experience.

Expertise Subject areas, industries, methodologies, focus topics
Experience Speaking history, roles, research, publications, awards
Visibility Assets Videos, interviews, articles, podcasts, press mentions
Available For Conferences, panels, workshops, podcasts, advisory roles

Opportunity Matching

Intelligent matching between experts and opportunities based on expertise, industry, language, region, experience level, availability, and topic relevance.

Conferences & keynotes Panel discussions Podcasts & interviews Workshops & masterclasses Advisory roles Teaching & lecturing Media appearances Book contributions

Visibility & Positioning Support

Structured guidance for building a public expert identity - from defining expertise areas to creating a full speaker portfolio.

  • Defining personal expertise areas and key topics
  • Writing compelling speaker bios (short, medium, and long versions)
  • Preparing talk abstracts and CFP submissions
  • Building a consistent public expert profile across platforms
  • Creating a speaker one-pager for organizer outreach
  • Developing a portfolio of visibility assets
  • Preparing for panels, interviews, and keynotes
  • Personal branding and thought leadership positioning
